About us

Contigo Language School is a language school based in Portmarnock. We currently offer Spanish, French, German and Catalan language classes. We cater for children and adults of all levels interested in learning a language and improving one's proficiency.

We are also introducing First Certificate Cambridge exams to prepare non native english speaking people for the B2 exam. Find out more about our courses.
